Frequently Asked Questions

What is SMS Varanasi?
SMS Varanasi is a private institute established in 1995 that has been granted Autonomous Status by UGC and 'A' Grade by NAAC. It is affiliated with AKTU and MGKVP and is ranked among the top 50 B-schools in India.
What programs are offered at SMS Varanasi?
SMS offers 7 premium programs: MBA, MCA, M.Com (Post Graduate – 2 Years), BBA, BCA, B.Com (Hons), and B.A. (Hons) Mass Communication (Under Graduate – 3 Years).
How are the placements at SMS Varanasi?
SMS Varanasi offers one of the most reputed placement records in North India. More than 50 Marketing, Finance, HR and IT companies visit campus, especially for MBA & MCA students. Alumni have been placed at TCS, DXC Technology, HDFC Bank, Wipro, and many other top companies globally.
Are admissions currently open?
Yes! Admissions are currently open for various UG and PG courses at SMS Varanasi for the 2026-27 academic session. Apply early to secure your spot and scholarship opportunities.
What accreditations does SMS Varanasi have?
SMS Varanasi is accredited by NAAC as 'A' Grade, approved by AICTE & UGC, granted Autonomous Status by UGC, and affiliated with AKTU (formerly UPTU) and MGKVP. It is recognized for excellence in education across India.
Does SMS offer international exposure?
Absolutely! SMS has International MOUs with top universities in the USA, Latin America, Europe & Asia. The institution offers Student & Faculty Exchange Programmes for global exposure, international conferences, and networking opportunities.
Is hostel & transport facility available?
Yes. SMS Varanasi provides hostel and transport facilities for students. The campus is located at Khushipur, P.O-Bachhaon, Varanasi-221011, Uttar Pradesh. Contact the helpline at 9161337733 for more details.
Are scholarships available at SMS Varanasi?
Yes! SMS Varanasi supports students through various scholarship schemes including UP State Scholarship and National Scholarship programs. Merit-based scholarships are also available for deserving students.
What makes SMS different from other colleges?
UGC has granted SMS the Prestigious Autonomous status. SMS has been relentlessly pursuing the path of growth and excellence with growing recognition as one of the top B-schools in India. The combination of academic excellence, international exposure, industry-oriented curriculum, and proven placements sets SMS apart.
